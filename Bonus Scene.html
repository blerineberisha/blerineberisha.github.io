                                    Athena's P.o.V.
Hiding the lightning was the easiest task I’ve ever given myself. Knowing Zeus would use his personal attachment to it to find it, made me think that I wouldn’t have to hide it too well and I was right! Zeus didn’t expect a thing,no one did. But since I’ve been planning all of this for weeks now, it’s a good thing they didn’t. Planting the “evidence” while “looking for it”, convincing them I was just investigating on my own and then blaming someone else for it - it was almost too easy. Maybe next time I should challenge myself a little more.
