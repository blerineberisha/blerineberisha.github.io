Chapter II

“Why are we here again?”, Dionysos asks after I had just explained to them what happened. “There were tiny red dotted stains on the floor where Zeus’ lightning was stolen, so we’re one of the suspect groups”, Hephaestus answers for me. “Yes”, I confirm, “now, moving onto the questions.. Where were you when the lightning was stolen?” “Last night I was meeting with Aphrodite”, Ares counters. I raise my eyebrow and smirk slightly, while Hephaestus clenches his fists. “Dearest Ares.. I never said when it happened.” His face turns red and Hephaestus relaxes a little. “Well,” Ares starts, “Aphrodite and I heard noises from Zeus’ room, but naturally we assumed it was just him and one of his … lovers going wild.” “Yeah,” Hermes says, “I saw Aphrodite and Ares met up at the main palace. I was delivering .. a little something for Zeus.” Taking notes of everything, I nod and observe the group. “So what were you doing, Hephaestus?”, I see him tense up again. “Iwas-Iwasworkinginmyshopalldaylong”, he mumbles, stumbling over his own words. “Alright, Hephaestus”, I say and he grunts. “So, Dionysos, that leaves you.” “Well”, Dionysos chuckles. “I don’t even remember what I did five minutes ago! I do think, however, I’d recall stealing lightning”, Dionysos smirks. Suddenly a flask appears in his hand. “Never have I ever been investigated for theft”, he says before taking another, long sip. “Um...”, I clear my throat. “Thank you for your time, gentlemen. I’ll let you know if there’s anything else I need”, I say before leaving.
