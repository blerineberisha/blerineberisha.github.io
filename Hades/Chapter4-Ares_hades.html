Ares

“So you were with Aphrodite?”, I ask Ares. “Yes”, he replies. He seems annoyed at me wanting to interview him. “And after that..?” He rolls his eyes. “I went to sleep, it’s kinda exhausting doing all the work, you know-”, he continues. “Okay, okay, I get it”, I interrupt him. “Did you see anything, though? Or hear anything?” “At around midnight we heard some noises from Zeus’ room, but as I said before, we thought that was Zeus himself”, he shakes his head. “I could’ve seen the thief, if I had gone to check on the noises.” He cringes, as if in pain. “But the thought of catching Zeus, quite literally, in the act, was not very appealing. I’m very glad I was… otherwise distracted.” “Alright”, I mumble. “Thank you for your time, Ares.”
