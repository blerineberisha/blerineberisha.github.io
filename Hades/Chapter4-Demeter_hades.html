Demeter

“I know you spent most of the day with Persephone”, I start. “What did you do after that, though?” Demeter is obviously annoyed. “I went to see how the nymphs had done in the mortal realm”, she says. “The last time I went away for too long the harvest was not as bountiful as usually. And several of my favorite tulips were… not optimally hydrated”, she finishes. I nod and take notes. “So you spent the day from morning to evening with Persephone and then you went to the mortal realm, until… ?” “I slept in the woods there. I shapeshifted into a chipmunk and slept in an underground burrow”, she says proudly. “As soon as the sun had risen this morning, I returned to the nymphs to let them know what needed more upkeep and then I returned to Olympus.” “Alright, thank you, Demeter”, I say with a smile.
