Hestia

“So, Hestia, you said you were at home all day?”, I ask as calmly as I can. “Yes!”, she almost screams. She is very nervous, always has been, but this whole lightning ordeal seems to be stressing her out even more. “What were you doing at home?” “I was just watching my favorite comfort shows! At some point Athena came over to see me, but she left rather quickly.” Hestia relaxes a little towards the end of her statement. “So what were you watching? When did Athena come over?”, I ask. “I was watching stand up comedy all morning and afternoon and Athena came over in the evening to check on me really quickly and then she left again”, Hestia explains, seeming to have calmed down a little. “I’m guessing if needed, I could check your browser history and ask Athena?”, I conclude. Hestia nods nervously. “Yes, sir!” “Alright, Hestia. Thank you for your time.”
