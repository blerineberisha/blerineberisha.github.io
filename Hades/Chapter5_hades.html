Chapter V

“Figured I’d find you here”, I say as I step onto a field of olive trees. I can see a smile form on Athena’s lips. “I knew you’d come to interview me too. Had to make it easy for you.” “Of course”, I smirk. “So, Athena, where-”, she interrupts me. “I went to see Hephaestus, Artemis and Hestia. They can clear me for the entire day”, she says. “Now”, she continues. “What clears you?” “I was in court all day in the underworld, there are several official files and dozens of witnesses”, I explain. Athena nods. “So who do you think it is?”
