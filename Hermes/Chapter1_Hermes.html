Chapter I

“I called you here, because... My lightning was stolen and as much as I hate to admit it, I can’t solve this by myself, since I’m very biased”, Zeus inhales and looks into my eyes. “Yes, I know. I’m very objective, blah blah”, i reply, while looking at the crime scene. There are three things that I notice; tiny red spots on the floor, a flower petal and .. no sign of breaking and entering. With the clues I can conclude that there are three groups that I can question. For the red stains, the suspects are Hermes, Hephaestos, Dionysus and Ares. For the flower petal the suspects are Aphrodite,Artemis, Apollo and Demeter. Because there are no signs of breaking and entering, it leaves a group of Zeus’ inner circle: Poseidon, Hera and Hestia.
Which clue would you like to focus on?
