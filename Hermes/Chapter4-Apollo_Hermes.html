Apollo

“You said you were on a date with a sea nymph?”, I ask Apollo. Apollo raises an eyebrow. “A forest nymph”, he corrects me. “I know you’re trying to catch me in a lie here”, he continues. “But firstly, it would not be that easy to fool me and secondly, I’m the god of the truth. If you can trust anyone’s word, it’s mine.” “So who were you on a date with?”, I ask him. “Daphne”, he replies with a slight smile on his face. “We were on our date from noon until evening and I’m infatuated”, he changes the subject. “Was that your first date?”, I ask, a bit weirded out. Apollo nods. “And it shall only get better from here!” I cringe a little. “Alright”, I say while writing down the new information. “Daphne and Artemis as alibis”, I mumble. “Thank you for your time, Apollo.”
