Dionysos

“So you don’t remember anything at all?”, I ask Dionysos. He just shakes his head and giggles. “Nothing!”, he exclaims and another flask appears. I snap it out of his hand. “Ooh, you’re joining? You’re more fun than I thought you wer-” I interrupt him by throwing the flask away. “Hey, I was gonna drink that!”, he says sadly. “You can drink later, first you need to think.” He sighs and mumbles “alright, but you owe me one!” A few seconds later his face lights up. “There were sea nymphs in my bed when I woke up! Maybe they remember something that can prove my innocence!” He takes out his phone and dials a number. “Hello darling! What did we do last night?”, he asks. “Well”, she starts, sounding very nervous. “At first we just drank and then we went to your room to-” “After that!”, Dionysos interrupts her, sounding very panicked and sober all of a sudden. “Oh, you fell asleep instantly. By the way, you snore really loudly. Do you have anything for that? Maybe you should get that checked out, I don’t think that’s normal. Maybe you should pay Hygieia a visit, I think she could-”, the sea nymph is once again interrupted. This time by Dionysos hanging up on her. “I think that’s enough. Thanks, Dionysos”, I say.
