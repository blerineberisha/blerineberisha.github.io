Poseidon

“So you went surfing for days?”, I ask Poseidon. “Yes! I had to make good use of the waves. There have rarely been such good ones.” I chuckle. “Aren’t you the one who makes the waves?”, I ask. “Well, yes”, he starts. “But that doesn’t mean I’m meeting my own standards for surfing.” I think for a second. “Is there anyone who can confirm your story?” “Several sea nymphs! They were rating my surfing”, Poseidon replies with a huge smile on his face. “You should’ve seen some of the fish I saw when I fell off my surfboard, they looked very exotic and fun! Some of them also looked very shocked to see me there-” “Thanks, Poseidon. That’d be all I need”, I interrupt him. Poseidon’s smile drops. “Tell me about all of that once I’m done with the investigation”, I add with a smile.
