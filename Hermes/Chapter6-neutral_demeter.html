Chapter VI

You accuse Demeter and you’re now explaining to Zeus what you found, Athena standing next to you. “I was able to clear everyone, except for Demeter”, I say. Athena nods. “Demeter’s story is completely unlikely. Her witnesses are unreliable”, Athena agrees. Zeus seems to be thinking. “If you’re both on the same page about this… you can’t be wrong, I guess.” He strokes his barely-there beard. “I’ll have to think of a punishment”, he says. “Can you prove this somehow, though? Besides just a flaw in her alibi?”, he asks. Athena thinks for a second. “We could see if we can find the lightning in her main temple”, she says. Zeus nods. “Let’s go.” And so we go to Demeter’s main temple. And there we find Zeus’ lightning. It wasn’t very easy to find, but since Zeus’ attachment to the lightning, it was a bit easier. Congrats! You found the thief… Are you sure about it being Demeter, though?
